Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is the website a streaming platform?

This website uses The Movie Database to search for movies and series, providing details. However, the actual streaming is facilitated through a third-party server. Please note that not all searched content may be available for streaming.

2. How does the Auto Update feature work?

Links are automatically updated with new or better quality as soon as they become available online.

3. Are links protected from DMCA?

Yes, our links are secure and protected to prevent removal due to DMCA notices.

4. Can I watch any movie or TV series I search for?

If the video is available on the server, you can watch it. However, there are over 50,000 movies available, and the collection keeps updating every minute.

Key Features

  • Responsive player that works on every device (Desktop, Mobile, Tablet).
  • High-quality links, mostly in 1080p resolution.
  • Fast streaming servers with a list of fastest options for users to choose from.
